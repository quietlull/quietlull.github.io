## Hey, I'm Rod

I'm a game developer and creative based in Vancouver. I started making games in 2023 after attending Vancouver Film School, but I've been playing and studying them my whole life.

This site is where I collect my work, my thoughts, and the things I find interesting — projects I've shipped, blog posts about game design, and the occasional deep dive into whatever I'm currently obsessed with.

## What I Do

I work across the full game development stack — design, code, and art — with a focus on mechanics that feel good to interact with. I'm drawn to games that have strong systemic design and games that use their medium to say something.

When I'm building something, I care about:

- **Feel** — how something responds when you touch it
- **Clarity** — whether the player ever has to guess
- **Cohesion** — whether the whole thing adds up to more than its parts

## Outside of Games

Before game dev I was chasing a career in competitive esports — I've held top-ranked slots in Starcraft 2, Hearthstone, Puyo Puyo Tetris, and a handful of others. That competitive mindset still informs how I approach design and improvement.

When I'm not at the computer I'm usually reading, watching anime, journaling, or thinking about something I recently played.
