  ### Hey There, I'm Rod

  I'm a technical artist focused on bridging the gap between art and engineering. I build shaders, tools, and rendering pipelines that help artists work faster and create more expressive visuals.

  I started game dev in 2023 at Vancouver Film School and have been deep in the weeds of real-time rendering ever since. From reverse-engineering production shaders to building automated sprite baking pipelines, I love the puzzle of making complex visual systems accessible.

  My toolkit spans HLSL/GLSL shaders, Blender Python scripting, Unreal materials, and custom tool development. I believe the best technical art is invisible — it just makes everything look better.
bio_more: |
  ### More About Me

  Before game dev I was chasing a career in competitive esports — I've held top-ranked slots in Starcraft 2, Hearthstone, Puyo Puyo Tetris, and more. That competitive drive for optimization carries directly into my technical work.

  When I'm not at the computer I'm usually reading, watching anime, journaling, or thinking about something I recently played. Feel free to explore my site, [check out my game design work](/game-design/), or read my [blog](/game-design/blogs/).
