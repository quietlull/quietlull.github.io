  ### Hey There, I'm Rod

  I'm a game designer who cares deeply about how games feel to play. I focus on mechanics that are intuitive, systems that reward curiosity, and experiences that stick with players after they put the controller down.

  I started making games in 2023 at Vancouver Film School, but I've been studying game design my whole life through thousands of hours of competitive play across dozens of genres. That player-first perspective informs everything I design.

  I believe great game design is about creating interesting decisions, clear feedback, and moments of surprise. Whether it's a shop management sim or an arena autobattler, I want every interaction to feel purposeful.
bio_more: |
  ### More About Me

  Before game dev I was chasing a career in competitive esports — I've held top-ranked slots in Starcraft 2, Hearthstone, Puyo Puyo Tetris, and more. Understanding games at a competitive level gives me a unique lens on balance, pacing, and player psychology.

  When I'm not at the computer I'm usually reading, watching anime, journaling, or thinking about something I recently played. Feel free to explore my site, [check out my technical art work](/tech-art/), or read my [ramblings](/ramblings/).
